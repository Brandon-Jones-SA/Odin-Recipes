Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

+

A quick and flavorful chicken and vegetable stir-fry that's perfect for busy weeknights. This dish combines tender chicken, colorful vegetables, and a savory stir-fry sauce for a delicious and healthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Cooked rice for serving

nstructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken with soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Mix well and let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add marinated chicken and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove chicken from the wok and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed. Saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add sliced onion, bell pepper, broccoli, and julienned carrots. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the cooked chicken to the wok and toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  5. Serve the chicken and vegetable stir-fry over cooked rice. Garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds if desired.

Enjoy this delicious and satisfying beef stew with crusty bread or over a bed of mashed potatoes!
