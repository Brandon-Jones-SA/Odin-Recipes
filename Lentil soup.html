Vegetarian Lentil Soup

+

A nourishing and hearty vegetarian lentil soup that's packed with protein and flavorful spices. This soup is easy to make, budget-friendly, and perfect for a wholesome lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups chopped kale or spinach
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Olive oil for drizzling

nstructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine lentils, diced onion, carrots, celery, garlic, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, coriander, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until lentils are tender.
  3. Stir in chopped kale or spinach and cook for an additional 5 minutes until the greens are wilted.
  4. Remove the pot from heat and stir in lemon juice. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  5. Ladle the vegetarian lentil soup into bowls, drizzle with olive oil, and serve with crusty bread.

Enjoy this delicious and satisfying beef stew with crusty bread or over a bed of mashed potatoes!
